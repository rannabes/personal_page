Abit of history!

I was born and raised in Tehran which is the capital city of Iran. I am happily married and have two beautiful daughters, Helena and Heava born on 2015 and 2019. I immigrated to Canada in 2010 to persue my PhD degree in quantum information science.

My passion about physics started in teenagehood, looking at the sky and wondering how the unverse was created and where the it ends. To feed my curiousity, I persued physics as an undergrad with the hope of discovering the mistery of unverse creation. But, when I was introduced to quantum mechanics and observed counter-intuitive behaviour of sub-atomic particles, I swinged my direction of curiousity from discovering macroscopic universe to understanding the microscopic world.

My graad school was exclusively focused on various areas in the field of quantum computation and quantum communication. I have worked on various projects, naming QKD for satellite communication, Nitrogen-Vacancy centers in diamonds, symmetry protected qubits and collective dynamics of open quantum systems.

After graduatin in 2016, I have worked for Xanadu Quantum Technologies in Toronto, Transformative Quangtum Technologies in Waterloo and Anyon Systems Inc. in Montreal, Canada. I am currentlt working for the government of Canada as quantum strategy advisor.

I am a social person and enjoy gathering with friends and family. My personal life has given me a special strength that helps me to be a wise and influencial mother, an inspiring friend and an advanced skill teacher.I enjoy educating, mentoring and training younger generations.
